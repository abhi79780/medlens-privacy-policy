MedLens Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

MedLens helps users analyze medicines and skincare products using AI. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

Information We Collect

  • Medicine scan history
  • Skincare scan history
  • Medicine reminder data
  • User account information (if signed in)

Camera Permission

The camera is used only to scan medicines and skincare products. Images are processed only for analysis.

Data Usage

Collected information is used to provide scan results, medicine reminders, and improve app functionality.

Third-Party Services

MedLens may use Firebase and AI services to provide analysis.

Medical Disclaimer

Analysis results are for informational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.
